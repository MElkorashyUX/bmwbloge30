How a BMW Can Improve your Life

The BMW E30 is the second generation of the 3 Series (1982–1994), widely regarded as an iconic, compact, rear-wheel-drive car that established BMW’s reputation for sporty handling. Available in sedan, coupe, convertible, and touring (wagon) styles, it is celebrated for its lightweight design, driver-focused interior, and robust straight-six engines (notably the 325i).
